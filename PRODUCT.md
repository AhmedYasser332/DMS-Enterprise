# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Accountants, managers, and administrative employees in an enterprise setting. They use this system daily for extended periods to archive documents, assign tasks, and track workflow progress.

## Product Purpose
An Enterprise Document Management System (DMS) that serves as the central hub for storing clients' legal entities, managing groups of records, and uploading/archiving critical attachments securely to Google Drive.

## Brand Personality
Professional, highly reliable, crisp, and distraction-free. The system should feel like a premium, native OS tool rather than a generic web app. It values speed and clarity over decorative flair.

## Anti-references
- The generic "Tailwind SaaS" look (bright blue on slate-50).
- Overly rounded corners (`border-radius: 24px+`).
- Excessive glassmorphism and heavy drop shadows ("ghost cards").
- Cluttered dashboards with low contrast text.

## Design Principles
1. **Content over chrome**: The UI should recede, letting the data and documents be the focus.
2. **Zero-latency feel**: Instant feedback and smooth, intentional motion without elasticity.
3. **Ergonomic for daily use**: High contrast, comfortable reading widths, and no eye strain for 8-hour sessions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast compliance for all text (≥4.5:1).
- Clear focus states for keyboard navigation.
- Accessible error states that do not rely solely on color.
